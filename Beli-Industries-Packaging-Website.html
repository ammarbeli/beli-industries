About Beli Industries CC

Founded by Imran Beli in Johannesburg, Beli Industries CC has established itself as a leading provider of plastic packaging solutions in South Africa. With over 15 years of industry experience, we've built our reputation on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Johannesburg allows us to produce high-quality plastic bags and packaging materials that meet international standards while serving the unique needs of the South African market.

Our Journey

2008

Company Founded

Imran Beli established Beli Industries CC in Johannesburg with a small team and a vision to provide quality packaging solutions.

2012

Expansion of Facilities

Moved to a larger manufacturing facility and invested in advanced printing technology to meet growing demand.

2016

Introduction of Eco-Friendly Products

Launched our first line of biodegradable and recyclable packaging options in response to environmental concerns.

2023

National Distribution Network

Established a nationwide distribution network to better serve clients across South Africa and neighboring countries.
